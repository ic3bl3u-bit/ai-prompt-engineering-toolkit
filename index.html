The framework

The RTFC framework

Most people write prompts like "write a blog post". The result: generic, mediocre output. RTFC fixes that with four mandatory components.

R

Role

What expert persona should the AI embody? Senior marketer, lawyer, embedded engineer…

T

Task

What concrete deliverable do you need? A 7-day campaign, a contract draft, a code review.

F

Format

How should the output be structured? Bullet list, table, JSON, markdown sections.

C

Constraints

What boundaries apply? Budget, length, tone, must-avoid, target audience.

A real example, before & after.

# Before — vague, gets generic output write a marketing plan for my app # After — RTFC, gets a usable plan You are a senior growth marketer with 10 years in B2B SaaS. Build a complete 30-day launch plan for [PRODUCT]. Format as a table: Day | Channel | Action | Metric | Owner. Constraints: - Budget: $5,000 total - Channels: email + LinkedIn + Product Hunt (no paid ads) - Avoid vanity metrics; track CAC and trial signups only
