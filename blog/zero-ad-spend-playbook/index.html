How I'm Building a Digital Product Business with $0 Ad Spend

The Setup

I sell AI workflow systems on Gumroad. Prices range from $29 to $149. My budget for ads: $0.

This is my exact traffic acquisition playbook — every channel, every tactic, zero paid tools.

Channel 1: GitHub Freemium Repo

The play: Give away 15 prompts for free on GitHub. Link to paid pack in README.

Why it works:

  • Developers browse GitHub daily
  • README is indexed by Google (free SEO)
  • Stars compound over time
  • "Free" + "MIT license" = trust

Cost: $0 · Time: 4 hours

Channel 2: Quora Answers

The play: Answer AI/ChatGPT questions on Quora. Link to products in bio.

Why it works:

  • Quora answers are indexed by Google and rank for years
  • Each answer is a permanent backlink
  • People searching "best ChatGPT prompts" are high intent
  • 15 answers → compounding traffic

Cost: $0 · Traffic quality: High (search intent)

Channel 3: Reddit Warmup

The play: Build karma in AI/productivity subreddits. Eventually share value posts.

Why it works:

  • Reddit hates self-promotion but loves genuine value
  • A text post that helps people → upvotes → visibility
  • r/ChatGPT has 5M+ members

Cost: $0 · Conversion: Low volume, high trust

Channel 4: Pinterest Boards

The play: Pin AI tips, prompt templates, and infographics. Link to Gumroad.

Why it works:

  • Pinterest is a visual search engine, not social media
  • Pins live forever (unlike tweets)
  • Business account = analytics

Cost: $0 · Long-term: Pins compound over months

Channel 5: SEO Content

The play: Write articles targeting AI prompt keywords.

Why it works:

  • Articles get indexed by Google fast
  • Permanent backlinks to GitHub repo → boosts repo SEO
  • Internal linking creates a web of entry points

Cost: $0

The Funnel

Free Traffic Sources ├── GitHub repo (15 free prompts) ├── Quora answers (15+ indexed) ├── Reddit value posts ├── Pinterest boards (79 pins) └── Blog articles │ ▼ Free Sample (email capture) │ ▼ Paid Products ($29 – $149) ├── Individual systems: $29 ├── Bundles: $47 ├── Complete toolkit: $79 └── Team license: $149

Key insight: Every channel feeds the funnel. No single channel is enough. But together, they create a web of entry points that compound over time.

TL;DR

You don't need ads to sell digital products. You need:

  1. A free tier (GitHub repo) that proves your quality
