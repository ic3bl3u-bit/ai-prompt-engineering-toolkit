How to Use These Prompts

Every prompt above follows the RTFC framework:

  • Role — Assigns a persona (changes tone + expertise)
  • Task — Defines the specific output needed
  • Format — Specifies structure (table, email, list, JSON)
  • Constraints — Sets boundaries (word count, tone, exclusions)

Customize the [BRACKETS] with your specific details. The more specific you are, the better the output.
